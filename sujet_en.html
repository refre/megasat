About MEGA Communications

MEGA Communications is your competent partner for all questions around the topic of telecommunications.

010012 Telecom GmbH

010012 Telecom GmbH was founded in 1998. Under the network access code digits 010012, it markets worldwide phone connections in the open Call by Call processes. The 010012 prefix dial service is one of the oldest, continuously independent prefix dial services in Germany.

You can find further information and rates on the 010012 homepage at http://www.010012.com/ and http://www.fonus.de/

11883 Telecom GmbH

Communication on all channels
Effective customer communication and tailor-made, innovative CRM as well as inbound and outbound solutions.

11883 Telecom GmbH was founded in 1999 and since that time, has developed into the third-largest provider for information and Call Center services in Europe.

Aside from Germany, the company is operationally active in Austria, Spain, Great Britain, Switzerland, Turkey and France and moves tens of millions of calls throughout Europe.

Under the national information number 11883, 11883 Telecom GmbH offers round-the-clock, comprehensive information and services as a multi-channel directory. With this, there is access to 250 Call Center Agents with a daily-updated 11883 database of just over 40 million participant entries and more than 400,000 additional commercial data and added value data.

You can find more information on the 11883 homepage at http://www.11883.com/

01057 Protel GmbH

01057 Protel GmbH was founded in 1997 and since then has offered a telecommunication service in Germany.

Since 2006, 01057 Protel GmbH has run its own telephone network for open Call by Call services. The Call by Call offers from 01057 Protel GmbH are usable with the network access code 01057.

You can find further information and rates at http://www.01057.com/
